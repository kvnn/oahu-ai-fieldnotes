II · Working with agents 23

By late 2024 the question had stopped being whether you could ship an agent. The question was whether the harness around it was opinionated enough to keep the agent honest.

Loose harnesses produced confident garbage. The pattern that kept working was small, fast loops with a verifier you actually trusted — not the model verifying itself.

The verifier is the opinion. It is what makes the harness yours.
